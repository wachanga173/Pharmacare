About Me

As Director of Patient Education & Support Services, I ensure patients understand every medication they receive and can follow treatment safely. I translate clinical instructions into clear, practical guidance across the full pharmacy journey.

I lead adherence communication, refill support pathways, and escalation channels that help patients stay informed and consistent with therapy while reducing confusion at home.
