About Me

As the Director of Drug Formulary Management at Pharmacare, I oversee the comprehensive pharmaceutical formulary system that powers our digital pharmacy platform. My expertise lies in medication classification, therapeutic category indexing, and drug inventory optimization. I ensure that patients and healthcare providers can easily discover and access the medications they need through intuitive filtering systems, therapeutic class organization, and formulary tier management.

I'm passionate about creating accessible, well-organized medication databases that enhance patient care. My role encompasses managing medication catalogs, implementing advanced search and filter functionalities, maintaining dosage strength ranges, coordinating with pharmaceutical suppliers, and ensuring our formulary meets the highest standards of accuracy and completeness for optimal medication discovery and patient safety.

Key Responsibilities

Drug Formulary Management

Manage the comprehensive pharmaceutical formulary database including medication catalogs, drug classifications, therapeutic categories, and formulary tiers. Ensure accurate medication information, pricing structures, and availability status across all pharmaceutical products.

Medication Classification Systems

Develop and maintain sophisticated medication classification systems based on therapeutic categories, drug classes, and pharmacological actions. Implement hierarchical taxonomies that enable efficient medication discovery and appropriate drug selection for healthcare providers.

Therapeutic Category Indexing

Create comprehensive therapeutic category indexes that organize medications by clinical use, disease treatment areas, and pharmacological effects. Implement advanced filtering systems that help patients and providers quickly find medications for specific health conditions.

Drug Inventory Management

Oversee drug inventory displays, stock level monitoring, and medication availability tracking. Coordinate with suppliers to maintain optimal formulary coverage, manage medication pricing updates, and ensure accurate product information across all pharmacy systems.

Dosage Strength Management

Manage dosage strength ranges and formulation varieties for all medications in the formulary. Ensure accurate representation of available strengths, package sizes, and administration routes to support appropriate medication selection and patient-specific dosing requirements.

Formulary Tier Management

Implement formulary tier sorting systems that organize medications by coverage levels, cost structures, and insurance plan preferences. Maintain tier designations, prior authorization requirements, and cost-sharing information to improve medication affordability and access.
