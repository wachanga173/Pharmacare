About Me

As Chief Dispensing Officer at Pharmacare, I oversee the final clinical and operational stage where verified prescriptions are safely released to patients. I coordinate dispensing checks, release sequencing, counseling handoff readiness, and workflow timing to maintain high reliability across every order.

I focus on patient safety, throughput consistency, and clear communication between pharmacists and patients. My role includes release-quality controls, peak-hour queue management, and operational escalation playbooks that keep medication handoffs accurate and traceable.

Key Responsibilities

Medication Release Validation

I enforce final release checks using barcode validation and order integrity controls before medicines are handed off.

Dispensing Safety Controls

I maintain clinical safety checkpoints that reduce release errors and protect patients during high-volume dispensing windows.

Queue Orchestration

I balance dispensing queues by priority and workload so urgent and routine orders are processed predictably.

SLA Performance Monitoring

I monitor turnaround targets and trigger operational responses to keep medication release times within service goals.

Counseling Handoff Readiness

I ensure pharmacists have complete context for patient counseling at pickup and release points.

Pickup Communication Flow

I manage readiness notifications and handoff communication so patients receive accurate release updates.
